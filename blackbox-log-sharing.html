Blackbox Logs

Share INAV Blackbox logs that are useful and privacy-aware.

Good logs make PID/filter recommendations easier to validate and compare across aircraft.

Include

  • Aircraft type, size, props, motors, battery, and firmware version.
  • PID and filter profile used for the flight.
  • Short description of the flight pattern and vibration symptoms.
  • Before/after logs when comparing a filter or PID change.

Remove Or Avoid

  • Private GPS/home-position data when logs are public.
  • Personal location notes, pilot identity, or sensitive flight areas.
  • Large unrelated files when screenshots or summary metrics are enough.
